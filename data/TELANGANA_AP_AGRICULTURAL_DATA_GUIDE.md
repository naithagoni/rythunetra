## 📍 LAND TYPES (5 Primary Types)

### 1. **Red Sandy Loam** (ఎర్ర ఇసుక బంకమట్టి)

- **Coverage:** 42% of Telangana
- **Districts:** Medak, Karimnagar, Warangal, Khammam, Nalgonda, Adilabad (parts)
- **Characteristics:** Well-drained, low water retention, pH 6.5-7.5, low organic carbon (0.3-0.5%)
- **Best for:** Cotton, Paddy (irrigated), Maize, Groundnut, Red Gram

### 2. **Black Cotton Soil / Vertisols** (నల్ల పత్తి నేల)

- **Coverage:** 28% of combined TS+AP region
- **Districts:** Adilabad, Nizamabad, Warangal, Nalgonda, Kurnool, Anantapur, Mahbubnagar
- **Characteristics:** High clay (40-60%), excellent moisture retention, pH 7.5-8.5
- **Best for:** Cotton, Soybean, Sorghum, Chickpea, Safflower

### 3. **Alluvial Deltaic Soil** (ఒండ్రు డెల్టా నేల)

- **Coverage:** Godavari & Krishna delta command areas
- **Districts:** East Godavari, West Godavari, Krishna, Guntur, NTR, Prakasam (coastal)
- **Characteristics:** Deep fertile, very high water holding, pH 6.5-7.8, high organic matter (1.0-2.5%)
- **Best for:** Paddy, Sugarcane, Banana, Coconut, Turmeric, Chilli, Vegetables

### 4. **Coastal Sandy Soil** (తీర ఇసుక నేల)

- **Coverage:** Coastal belt
- **Districts:** Srikakulam, Vizianagaram, Visakhapatnam, East Godavari (coastal)
- **Characteristics:** Coarse texture, very low water retention, pH 6.0-7.2, very low organic matter (<0.3%)
- **Best for:** Groundnut, Cashew, Coconut, Casuarina, Pulses

### 5. **Red Laterite Upland Soil** (ఎర్ర లేటరైట్ ఎత్తైన ప్రాంత నేల)

- **Coverage:** Agency areas & high rainfall belts
- **Districts:** Vizianagaram (Agency), Srikakulam (Agency), Khammam (hilly), Bhadrachalam
- **Characteristics:** Acidic pH (5.0-6.5), high iron, low fertility, prone to erosion
- **Best for:** Finger Millet, Cashew, Mango, Tamarind, Turmeric (with amendments)

## 🧪 ORGANIC REMEDIES (14 Evidence-Based Options)

### 🍄 BIO-FUNGICIDES

#### 1. **Trichoderma viride** (ట్రైకోడెర్మా వైరిడి)

- **Application:** Seed treatment @ 10g/kg; Soil @ 2kg/acre with FYM
- **Target:** Seedling blight, Root rot, Wilt, Damping off
- **Mode:** Antagonistic fungi, competes for nutrients, produces antibiotics
- **Shelf Life:** 180 days

#### 2. **Pseudomonas fluorescens** (స్యూడోమోనాస్ ఫ్లోరెసెన్స్)

- **Application:** Seed @ 10g/kg; Foliar @ 10g/L water
- **Target:** Bacterial leaf blight, fungal leaf spots
- **Mode:** Competes on leaf surface, induces systemic resistance
- **Shelf Life:** 60 days

#### 3. **Bacillus subtilis** (బాసిలస్ సబ్‌టిలిస్)

- **Application:** Foliar spray @ 5g/L; Soil drench
- **Target:** Blasts, blights, soft rots
- **Mode:** Produces antimicrobial lipopeptides
- **Shelf Life:** 90 days

### 🌿 BOTANICAL EXTRACTS

#### 4. **Neem Oil Emulsion** (వేపనూనె ఎమల్షన్)

- **Application:** 5-10 ml/L with liquid soap emulsifier
- **Target:** Aphids, whiteflies, thrips, powdery mildew
- **Mode:** Repellent, anti-feedant, growth regulator
- **Shelf Life:** Prepare fresh each time (max 2-3 days)

#### 5. **Panchagavya** (పంచగవ్య)

- **Application:** 3% spray (30ml/L) every 15 days
- **Target:** General plant immunity, pest deterrent
- **Ingredients:** Cow dung, urine, milk, curd, ghee, jaggery, banana
- **Shelf Life:** 15 days after fermentation complete

#### 6. **Neem Seed Kernel Extract (NSKE)** (వేప గింజ సారం)

- **Application:** 5% spray (50g powder/L, overnight soak, filter)
- **Target:** Leaf eating caterpillars, pod borers, aphids
- **Mode:** Azadirachtin acts as feeding deterrent
- **Shelf Life:** Use fresh extract within 8 hours

### 🥬 PLANT EXTRACTS

#### 7. **Garlic-Chilli Extract** (వెల్లుల్లి-మిరపకాయ సారం)

- **Application:** 50g garlic + 50g chilli/L, boil, cool, filter, spray
- **Target:** Aphids, thrips, mites, caterpillars
- **Mode:** Repellent sulfur compounds and capsaicin
- **Shelf Life:** Use within 24 hours

#### 8. **Tobacco Decoction** (పొగాకు కషాయం)

- **Application:** 250g tobacco dust boiled in 5L water, dilute 1:5
- **Target:** Sucking pests (aphids, jassids, thrips)
- **Mode:** Nicotine acts as neurotoxin to insects
- **Shelf Life:** 3-4 days
- **Caution:** Don't use on solanaceous crops (tomato, brinjal, chilli)

### ⚗️ TRADITIONAL FUNGICIDES (Organic-Approved)

#### 9. **Bordeaux Mixture** (బోర్డో మిశ్రమం)

- **Formula:** 1% solution = 1kg CuSO₄ + 1kg lime in 100L water
- **Application:** Preventive spray before rains
- **Target:** Blights, leaf spots, canker, downy mildew
- **Mode:** Copper ions disrupt fungal cell membranes
- **Shelf Life:** Use same day (do not store)

#### 10. **Cow Urine + Neem** (గోముత్ర + వేప మిశ్రమం)

- **Application:** 10L cow urine + 1kg neem leaves, ferment 10 days, dilute 1:10
- **Target:** Fungal diseases, sucking pests
- **Mode:** Ammonia and neem compounds synergize
- **Shelf Life:** 30 days after fermentation

### 🦠 BIO-PESTICIDES

#### 11. **Beauveria bassiana** (బ్యూవేరియా బాసియానా)

- **Application:** 5g/L spray in evening (avoid heat)
- **Target:** White grubs, termites, beetles, stem borers
- **Mode:** Fungal pathogen infects and kills insects
- **Shelf Life:** 120 days

#### 12. **Metarhizium anisopliae** (మెటారిజియం అనిసోప్లియా)

- **Application:** 5g/L soil drench or spray
- **Target:** White grubs, root weevils, termites
- **Mode:** Fungal entomopathogen
- **Shelf Life:** 120 days

### 🥛 NUTRIENT & IMMUNITY BOOSTERS

#### 13. **Jeevamrutham** (జీవామృతం)

- **Application:** Soil drench @ 200L/acre or 3% foliar spray
- **Ingredients:** Cow dung 10kg, cow urine 10L, jaggery 2kg, pulse flour 2kg, soil 1kg, water 200L
- **Purpose:** Soil health, microbial boost, plant vigor
- **Shelf Life:** Use within 7 days of preparation

#### 14. **Vermiwash** (వర్మీవాష్)

- **Application:** Foliar spray @ 10% (100ml/L every 15 days)
- **Purpose:** Nutrient supplement, growth promoter, pest deterrent
- **Preparation:** Collect leachate from vermicompost unit
- **Shelf Life:** 15 days if stored in cool place

## 🐛 MAJOR DISEASES BY CROP & LAND TYPE

### 🌾 PADDY (వరి)

#### **On Alluvial Deltaic Soil**

**1. Blast Disease (బ్లాస్ట్ వ్యాధి)**

- **Pathogen:** _Pyricularia oryzae_ (fungus)
- **Symptoms:** Spindle-shaped lesions on leaves with gray center & brown margins; neck infection causes panicle collapse
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity (>90%), cool nights (20-25°C), excess nitrogen
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment
    2. Pseudomonas foliar spray
    3. Reduce nitrogen in disease-prone areas

**2. Bacterial Leaf Blight** (బాక్టీరియల్ ఆకు కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Xanthomonas oryzae pv. oryzae_
- **Symptoms:** Water-soaked lesions starting from leaf tips, yellow halos, wilting
- **Favorable Conditions:** Warm humid weather, high nitrogen, water logging
- **Remedies:**
    1. Pseudomonas fluorescens seed treatment + spray
    2. Bordeaux mixture spray
    3. Avoid water stagnation

**3. Sheath Blight** (ఆవరణ కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Rhizoctonia solani_ (fungus)
- **Symptoms:** Oval greenish-gray lesions on leaf sheath near water line, ladder pattern on stems
- **Favorable Conditions:** Dense planting, high humidity, stagnant water
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bacillus subtilis spray
    2. Jeevamrutham soil application
    3. Improve drainage, reduce plant density

**4. Brown Spot** (గోధుమ మచ్చల వ్యాధి)

- **Pathogen:** _Bipolaris oryzae_ (fungus)
- **Symptoms:** Round brown spots on leaves, grains, glumes; poor grain filling
- **Favorable Conditions:** Nutrient deficiency, drought stress
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment
    2. Balanced fertilization
    3. Panchagavya spray for nutrition

**5. False Smut** (తప్పుడు పొగమంచు)

- **Pathogen:** _Ustilaginoidea virens_ (fungus)
- **Symptoms:** Individual grains replaced by velvety greenish-black spore balls
- **Favorable Conditions:** Flowering stage rain, high humidity
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture at boot leaf stage
    2. Pseudomonas spray at panicle initiation
    3. Avoid excessive nitrogen

#### **On Black Cotton Soil**

_[Same diseases as Alluvial + additional drought stress and wilt issues]_

**6. Root Rot** (వేరు కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Fusarium_, _Pythium_ spp.
- **Symptoms:** Stunted growth, yellowing, blackening of roots, easy uprooting
- **Favorable Conditions:** Water logging in heavy clay soil
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma soil application
    2. Improved drainage
    3. Jeevamrutham soil treatment

#### **On Red Sandy Loam**

_[Same as above + nutrient deficiency stress-related diseases]_

### 🧶 COTTON (పత్తి)

#### **On Black Cotton Soil**

**1. Wilt Disease** (వాడిపోయే వ్యాధి)

- **Pathogen:** _Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. vasinfectum_
- **Symptoms:** Sudden wilting, yellowing of leaves, vascular browning, plant death
- **Favorable Conditions:** Temperature 25-30°C, soil pH > 7.0
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment + soil application
    2. Pseudomonas seed treatment
    3. Intercropping with sorghum or maize
    4. Avoid infected field rotation

**2. Alternaria Leaf Spot** (ఆల్టర్నేరియా ఆకు మచ్చలు)

- **Pathogen:** _Alternaria macrospora_
- **Symptoms:** Circular brown spots with concentric rings on leaves, premature defoliation
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, moderate temperature
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray
    2. Pseudomonas foliar spray
    3. Panchagavya for plant vigor

**3. Grey Mildew** (బూడిద ముడత)

- **Pathogen:** _Ramularia areola_
- **Symptoms:** White/grey powdery growth on lower leaf surface, angular lesions
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, dense canopy
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray
    2. Bacillus subtilis
    3. Improve air circulation

**4. Bacterial Blight** (బాక్టీరియల్ కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Xanthomonas citri pv. malvacearum_
- **Symptoms:** Angular water-soaked lesions, black arm on stem, boll rot
- **Favorable Conditions:** Monsoon rains, high humidity
- **Remedies:**
    1. Pseudomonas seed treatment + spray
    2. Bordeaux mixture
    3. Avoid overhead irrigation

**5. Root Rot** (వేరు కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Rhizoctonia solani_, _Macrophomina phaseolina_
- **Symptoms:** Blackening of roots, stunted growth, yellowing, wilting
- **Favorable Conditions:** Water logging + heat stress (>35°C)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed + soil treatment
    2. Jeevamrutham for soil health
    3. Avoid water stagnation

#### **On Red Sandy Loam**

_[Similar diseases + drought stress complications, thrips/sucking pest-vectored diseases more common]_

**6. Leaf Curl Virus** (ఆకు వంకర వైరస్)

- **Pathogen:** Cotton leaf curl virus (Whitefly-transmitted)
- **Symptoms:** Upward/downward leaf curling, vein thickening, stunting
- **Vector:** Whitefly (_Bemisia tabaci_)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray for whitefly control
    2. Garlic-chilli extract
    3. Remove infected plants
    4. Use resistant varieties (like Bt cotton, Desi varieties)

### 🌽 MAIZE (మొక్కజొన్న)

#### **On Red Sandy Loam**

**1. Turcicum Leaf Blight** (టర్సికమ్ ఆకు కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Exserohilum turcicum_
- **Symptoms:** Long elliptical tan lesions on leaves, reduced photosynthesis
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, moderate temperature
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray
    2. Pseudomonas foliar spray
    3. Crop rotation with non-hosts
    4. Balanced nutrition

**2. Maydis Leaf Blight** (మేడిస్ ఆకు కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Bipolaris maydis_
- **Symptoms:** Rectangular tan lesions between veins, premature drying
- **Favorable Conditions:** Warm humid weather
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment
    2. Bacillus subtilis spray
    3. Remove infected debris

**3. Common Rust** (సాధారణ తుప్పు)

- **Pathogen:** _Puccinia sorghi_
- **Symptoms:** Reddish-brown pustules on both leaf surfaces
- **Favorable Conditions:** Cool humid conditions, dew
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil + cow urine spray
    2. Panchagavya for plant vigor
    3. Avoid late sowing

**4. Stalk Rot** (కాండ కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Fusarium_, _Macrophomina_ spp.
- **Symptoms:** Soft rotting of lower internodes, lodging, poor grain fill
- **Favorable Conditions:** Water logging followed by drought stress
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma soil application
    2. Jeevamrutham
    3. Proper drainage, balanced K nutrition

**5. Post-Flowering Stalk Rot** (పూత తర్వాత కాండ కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Fusarium moniliforme_, _Macrophomina phaseolina_
- **Symptoms:** Stalk rot after flowering, lodging, shriveled grains
- **Favorable Conditions:** Drought + heat stress during grain filling
- **Remedies:**
    1. Adequate irrigation during reproductive stage
    2. Balanced K and Zn nutrition
    3. Avoid plant stress

### 🌶️ CHILLI (మిరపకాయ)

#### **On Alluvial Deltaic Soil**

**1. Damping Off** (మొలక కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Pythium_, _Rhizoctonia_ spp.
- **Symptoms:** Seed/seedling rot in nursery, collar region water soaking, toppling
- **Favorable Conditions:** High moisture, poor drainage in nursery
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment
    2. Raised bed nursery with good drainage
    3. Avoid over-watering

**2. Anthracnose (Fruit Rot)** (యాంథ్రాక్‌నోజ్ పండ్ల కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Colletotrichum capsici_
- **Symptoms:** Sunken circular spots on fruits, concentric rings, fruit rot
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, fruit maturity stage, rainy season
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray
    2. Bacillus subtilis spray
    3. Harvest before over-maturity
    4. Avoid fruit touching soil

**3. Powdery Mildew** (పొడి ముడత)

- **Pathogen:** _Leveillula taurica_
- **Symptoms:** White powdery patches on leaves, leaf drop, reduced yield
- **Favorable Conditions:** Dry weather with high humidity at night
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray (5ml/L)
    2. Cow urine + neem fermented spray
    3. Panchagavya 3%

**4. Bacterial Wilt** (బాక్టీరియల్ వాడిపోవుట)

- **Pathogen:** _Ralstonia solanacearum_
- **Symptoms:** Sudden wilting without yellowing, vascular browning
- **Favorable Conditions:** Warm soil (28-32°C), high moisture
- **Remedies:**
    1. Pseudomonas seed + soil treatment
    2. Bordeaux mixture drench
    3. Crop rotation with non-hosts (cereals)
    4. Remove infected plants immediately

**5. Leaf Curl Virus** (ఆకు వంకర వైరస్)

- **Pathogen:** Chilli leaf curl virus (Whitefly-vectored)
- **Symptoms:** Severe leaf curling, puckering, stunting, flower drop
- **Vector:** Whitefly
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray for vector control
    2. Garlic-chilli extract
    3. Yellow sticky traps
    4. Remove infected plants
    5. Use virus-free seedlings

**6. Cercospora Leaf Spot** (సెర్కోస్పోరా ఆకు మచ్చలు)

- **Pathogen:** _Cercospora capsici_
- **Symptoms:** Small brown spots with gray center on leaves
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, warm temperature
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture
    2. Pseudomonas spray
    3. Proper spacing for air circulation

### 🟡 TURMERIC (పసుపు)

**2. Leaf Blotch** (ఆకు మచ్చలు)

- **Pathogen:** _Taphrina maculans_
- **Symptoms:** Small brown spots enlarging to irregular blotches, leaf yellowing
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, monsoon season
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray
    2. Pseudomonas foliar spray
    3. Remove infected leaves

**3. Leaf Spot** (ఆకు మచ్చ వ్యాధి)

- **Pathogen:** _Colletotrichum capsici_
- **Symptoms:** Small oval brown spots, premature leaf fall
- **Favorable Conditions:** Rainy season, dense canopy
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bacillus subtilis spray

    3. Panchagavya for immunity

### 🥜 GROUNDNUT (వేరుశనగ)

#### **On Red Sandy Loam**

**1. Tikka Disease / Early Leaf Spot** (టిక్కా వ్యాధి)

- **Pathogen:** _Cercospora arachidicola_
- **Symptoms:** Small circular dark brown spots on upper leaf surface, yellowing, defoliation
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, warm temperature
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray
    2. Pseudomonas spray
    3. Crop rotation

**2. Late Leaf Spot** (ఆలస్య ఆకు మచ్చలు)

- **Pathogen:** _Phaeoisariopsis personata_
- **Symptoms:** Larger dark brown to black spots, severe defoliation
- **Favorable Conditions:** Late season high humidity
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture
    2. Bacillus subtilis
    3. Maintain field sanitation

**3. Rust** (తుప్పు)

- **Pathogen:** _Puccinia arachidis_
- **Symptoms:** Orange-brown pustules on lower leaf surface
- **Favorable Conditions:** Cool humid weather
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray
    2. Cow urine + neem
    3. Balanced nutrition (K, Zn)

**4. Collar Rot / Stem Rot** (కాలర్ కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Aspergillus niger_, _Sclerotium rolfsii_
- **Symptoms:** Water soaked lesions at collar region, white mycelial growth, wilting
- **Favorable Conditions:** High soil moisture + temperature
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed + soil treatment
    2. Pseudomonas
    3. Avoid water logging, deep sowing

**5. Bud Necrosis Virus** (మొగ్గ నెక్రోసిస్ వైరస్)

- **Pathogen:** Groundnut bud necrosis virus (Thrips-vectored)
- **Symptoms:** Necrotic spots on young leaves, stunting, yield loss
- **Vector:** Thrips
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray for thrips control
    2. Tobacco decoction
    3. Remove infected plants
    4. Barrier crops (maize, sorghum)

#### **On Coastal Sandy Soil**

_[Same diseases but more severe due to nutrient stress; add micronutrient sprays]_

### 🫘 RED GRAM / PIGEON PEA (కందులు)

#### **On Red Sandy Loam**

**1. Fusarium Wilt** (ఫ్యూసేరియమ్ వాడిపోవుట)

- **Pathogen:** _Fusarium udum_
- **Symptoms:** Yellowing of leaves, vascular browning, sudden wilting
- **Favorable Conditions:** Warm soil (25-30°C), acidic pH
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed + soil treatment
    2. Pseudomonas seed treatment
    3. Crop rotation with cereals
    4. Use wilt-resistant varieties

**2. Sterility Mosaic Disease** (స్టెరిలిటీ మొజైక్ వ్యాధి)

- **Pathogen:** Sterility mosaic virus (Mite-vectored)
- **Symptoms:** Mosaic pattern, partial/complete sterility, no pod formation
- **Vector:** Eriophyid mite
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray for mite control
    2. Remove infected plants immediately
    3. Border crops as barrier
    4. Use resistant varieties

**3. Phytophthora Blight** (ఫైటోప్థోరా కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Phytophthora drechsleri_
- **Symptoms:** Water soaked lesions on stem, leaves; sudden plant death
- **Favorable Conditions:** Water logging, high rainfall
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma soil treatment
    2. Improved drainage
    3. Bordeaux mixture drench
    4. Avoid water stagnation

**4. Alternaria Blight** (ఆల్టర్నేరియా కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Alternaria alternata_
- **Symptoms:** Concentric rings on leaves, pods; premature defoliation
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray
    2. Bacillus subtilis
       3 Panchagavya

**5. Pod Borer Complex** (కాయల పురుగులు)

- **Pest:** _Helicoverpa armigera_, _Maruca_ spp.
- **Damage:** Bore into pods, feed on developing seeds
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem seed kernel extract 5%
    2. _Beauveria bassiana_ spray
    3. Garlic-chilli extract
    4. Bt spray (organic _Bacillus thuringiensis_)

### 🍅 TOMATO (టమాట)

#### **On Alluvial Deltaic Soil**

**1. Early Blight** (ముందస్తు కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Alternaria solani_
- **Symptoms:** Concentric ring pattern on leaves, stem lesions, fruit rot
- **Favorable Conditions:** Warm humid weather
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray
    2. Bacillus subtilis
    3. Crop rotation
    4. Mulching to prevent soil splash

**2. Late Blight** (ఆలస్య కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Phytophthora infestans_
- **Symptoms:** Water soaked lesions on leaves, white mycelial growth on lower surface, rapid plant death
- **Favorable Conditions:** Cool humid weather, night dew
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture (preventive spray before rains)
    2. Pseudomonas spray
    3. Improve air circulation
    4. Avoid overhead irrigation

**3. Bacterial Wilt** (బాక్టీరియల్ వాడిపోవుట)

- **Pathogen:** _Ralstonia solanacearum_
- **Symptoms:** Sudden wilting, vascular browning, bacterial ooze
- **Favorable Conditions:** Warm soil, high moisture
- **Remedies:**
    1. Pseudomonas seed treatment + soil drench
    2. Bordeaux mixture
    3. Crop rotation with non-solanaceous crops
    4. Solarization

**4. Leaf Curl Virus** (ఆకు వంకర వైరస్)

- **Pathogen:** Tomato leaf curl virus (Whitefly-vectored)
- **Symptoms:** Severe leaf curling, stunting, flower drop, poor fruiting
- **Vector:** Whitefly
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray for whitefly control
    2. Yellow sticky traps
    3. Garlic-chilli extract
    4. Remove infected plants
    5. Use resistant varieties

**5. Septoria Leaf Spot** (సెప్టోరియా ఆకు మచ్చలు)

- **Pathogen:** _Septoria lycopersici_
- **Symptoms:** Small circular spots with dark margins on lower leaves
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, moderate temperature
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture
    2. Pseudomonas spray
    3. Remove lower infected leaves

**6. Buckeye Rot** (బక్‌ఐ కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Phytophthora nicotianae_
- **Symptoms:** Concentric ring pattern on fruit, starts from bottom touching soil
- **Favorable Conditions:** Rainy season, fruits touching soil
- **Remedies:**
    1. Mulching to prevent soil-fruit contact
    2. Bordeaux mixture spray
    3. Staking plants
    4. Improve drainage

### 🌾 SUGARCANE (చెరకు)

#### **On Alluvial Deltaic Soil**

**1. Red Rot** (ఎర్ర కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Colletotrichum falcatum_
- **Symptoms:** Red discoloration of internal tissue with white patches, sour smell
- **Favorable Conditions:** High moisture, temperature 25-30°C
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma sett treatment
    2. Use healthy setts from disease-free areas
    3. Crop rotation
    4. Resistant varieties

**2. Smut** (నల్లమచ్చల వ్యాధి)

- **Pathogen:** _Sporisorium scitamineum_
- **Symptoms:** "Whip-like" structure with black spores emerging from top
- **Favorable Conditions:** Dry weather during sprouting
- **Remedies:**
    1. Remove and burn smut whips before spore release
    2. Trichoderma sett treatment
    3. Resistant varieties

**3. Wilt Disease** (వాడిపోవుట)

- **Pathogen:** Various (_Fusarium_, _Pythium_, _Ceratocystis_)
- **Symptoms:** Yellowing, drying from top, internal discoloration
- **Favorable Conditions:** Water stress, poor drainage
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma + Pseudomonas sett treatment
    2. Proper drainage
    3. Jeevamrutham soil health
    4. Avoid ratoon cropping in infected fields

**4. Leaf Scald** (ఆకు దహనం)

- **Pathogen:** _Xanthomonas albilineans_
- **Symptoms:** White pencil-like streaks on leaves, wilting
- **Favorable Conditions:** High rainfall, wounds
- **Remedies:**
    1. Pseudomonas sett treatment
    2. Remove infected plants
    3. Use disease-free setts
    4. Avoid mechanical injury

### 🍌 BANANA (అరటి)

#### **On Alluvial Deltaic Soil**

**1. Panama Wilt / Fusarium Wilt** (ఫ్యూసేరియమ్ వాడిపోవుట)

- **Pathogen:** _Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. cubense_
- **Symptoms:** Yellowing of older leaves, splitting of pseudostem, plant death
- **Favorable Conditions:** Acidic soil, poor drainage
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma + Pseudomonas soil application
    2. Tissue culture disease-free plants
    3. Crop rotation with non-hosts
    4. Drain water properly

**2. Sigatoka Leaf Spot** (సిగటోకా ఆకు మచ్చలు)

- **Pathogen:** _Mycosphaerella musicola_
- **Symptoms:** Small brown spots enlarging with yellow halos, premature leaf drying
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, warm temperature
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray
    2. Pseudomonas spray
    3. Remove old infected leaves
    4. Proper spacing

**3. Bunchy Top Virus** (బంచీ టాప్ వైరస్)

- **Pathogen:** Banana bunchy top virus (Aphid-vectored)
- **Symptoms:** Dark green streaks on petiole, bunchy appearance, stunted plant
- **Vector:** Banana aphid
- **Remedies:**
    1. Remove infected plants immediately and destroy
    2. Use virus-free planting material
    3. Neem oil spray for aphid control
    4. Barrier cropping

**4. Rhizome Rot** (వేళ్లు కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Pectobacterium carotovorum_
- **Symptoms:** Water soaked, soft rotting of rhizome, foul smell
- **Favorable Conditions:** Water logging
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma rhizome treatment
    2. Improved drainage
    3. Avoid injury to rhizome
    4. Bordeaux paste on wounds
