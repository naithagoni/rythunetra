## 🍄 BIO-FUNGICIDES (Microbial Agents)

### 1. **Trichoderma viride** (ట్రైకోడెర్మా వైరిడి)

- **Application:** Seed @ 10g/kg; Soil @ 2kg/acre + FYM; Seedling dip @ 10g/L
- **Target:** Damping off, Root rot, Wilt, Stem rot, Collar rot
- **Mode:** Mycoparasitism, antibiosis, competition, induced resistance
- **Dosage Details:**
    - Seed treatment: 10g per kg seed
    - Soil application: 2.5 kg per acre mixed with 100 kg FYM
    - Seedling root dip: 10g per liter, 30-minute dip before transplanting
- **Shelf Life:** 180 days (store below 30°C)
- **Cost:** ₹40-60 per 100g packet
- **Availability:** All agricultural input shops

### 2. **Trichoderma harzianum** (ട్రైకోడెర్మా హార్జియానమ్)

- **Application:** Similar to T. viride but more effective against Fusarium
- **Target:** Fusarium wilt, Root diseases, Fruit rots
- **Mode:** Stronger mycoparasitic activity than T. viride
- **Dosage:** Same as T. viride
- **Shelf Life:** 180 days
- **Cost:** ₹45-70 per 100g

### 3. **Pseudomonas fluorescens** (స్యూడోమోనాస్ ఫ్లోరెసెన్స్)

- **Application:**
    - Seed: 10g/kg seed
    - Soil: 2.5 kg/acre with FYM
    - Foliar: 10g/L water, spray every 10-15 days
- **Target:** Bacterial blights, bacterial leaf spots, fungal leaf diseases
- **Mode:** Siderophore production, HCN production, induced systemic resistance
- **Shelf Life:** 60 days (refrigerate if possible)
- **Cost:** ₹50-80 per 100g

### 4. **Bacillus subtilis** (బాసిలస్ సబ్‌టిలిస్)

- **Application:** Foliar 5g/L; seed treatment 8g/kg; soil drench 10g/L
- **Target:** Bacterial and fungal blights, soft rots, damping off
- **Mode:** Lipopeptide antibiotics (surfactin, iturin, fengycin)
- **Shelf Life:** 90 days
- **Cost:** ₹60-90 per 100g

### 5. **Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt)** (బాసిలస్ థురింజెన్సిస్)

- **Type:** Bio-insecticide (but affects some fungal growth too)
- **Application:** 1-2 g/L spray on foliage
- **Target:** Lepidopteran larvae (caterpillars, pod borers, fruit borers)
- **Mode:** Produces crystal proteins toxic to insect larvae
- **Shelf Life:** 120 days
- **Cost:** ₹100-150 per 100g

### 6. **Beauveria bassiana** (బ్యూవేరియా బాసియానా)

- **Application:** 5g/L spray in evening (avoid hot sun)
- **Target:** White grubs, beetles, stem borers, thrips, whiteflies
- **Mode:** Fungal entomopathogen - penetrates insect cuticle
- **Shelf Life:** 120 days
- **Application Note:** Evening application essential (UV degrades spores)
- **Cost:** ₹80-120 per 100g

### 7. **Metarhizium anisopliae** (మెటారిజియం అనిసోప్లియా)

- **Application:** 5g/L soil drench or foliar spray
- **Target:** White grubs, termites, root weevils, grasshoppers
- **Mode:** Entomopathogenic fungus
- **Shelf Life:** 120 days
- **Cost:** ₹80-120 per 100g

### 8. **Paecilomyces lilacinus** (పేసిలోమైసెస్ లిలాసినస్)

- **Application:** Soil @ 2kg/acre; seedling dip
- **Target:** Root-knot nematodes, cyst nematodes
- **Mode:** Parasitizes nematode eggs and females
- **Shelf Life:** 150 days
- **Cost:** ₹100-150 per 100g

### 9. **Verticillium lecanii** (వర్టిసిలియం లెకానీ)

- **Application:** 5-8g/L foliar spray
- **Target:** Aphids, whiteflies, thrips, rust fungi
- **Mode:** Dual action - entomopathogen + mycoparasite
- **Shelf Life:** 90 days
- **Cost:** ₹90-130 per 100g

### 10. **Ampelomyces quisqualis** (ఆమ్పెలోమైసెస్ క్విస్క్వాలిస్)

- **Application:** 5g/L spray at powdery mildew initiation
- **Target:** Powdery mildew fungi (hyperparasite)
- **Mode:** Parasitizes powdery mildew mycelia
- **Shelf Life:** 120 days
- **Cost:** ₹120-180 per 100g

## 🌿 BOTANICAL EXTRACTS & OILS

### 11. **Neem Oil (Cold-Pressed)** (వేపనూనె)

- **Application:** 5-10 ml/L + 1ml liquid soap emulsifier
- **Target:** Aphids, whiteflies, thrips, mites, jassids, powdery mildew
- **Active:** Azadirachtin, nimbin, salannin
- **Mode:** IGR (insect growth regulator), anti-feedant, repellent
- **Shelf Life:** Fresh preparation (use within 24 hours)
- **Cost:** ₹200-350 per liter
- **Application Timing:** Early morning or evening (avoid hot sun)

### 12. **Neem Seed Kernel Extract (NSKE)** (వేప గింజ సారం)

- **Preparation:** 50g neem seed powder/L water, overnight soak, filter
- **Application:** 5% spray
- **Target:** Leaf eating caterpillars, pod borers, fruit borers, aphids
- **Active:** Higher azadirachtin than neem oil
- **Shelf Life:** Use within 6-8 hours of preparation
- **Cost:** ₹100-150 per kg powder

### 13. **Neem Cake** (వేప పిండి)

- **Application:** 200-250 kg/acre soil incorporation
- **Target:** Soil-borne diseases, nematodes, termites; also acts as fertilizer
- **Mode:** Slow-release azadirachtin + NPK (5-1-2)
- **Shelf Life:** 6-12 months
- **Cost:** ₹20-30 per kg

### 14. **Karanj Oil (Pongamia)** (కరంజ నూనె)

- **Application:** 10ml/L with emulsifier
- **Target:** Similar to neem - sucking pests, leaf miners
- **Active:** Karanjin, pongamol

- **Shelf Life:** Fresh preparation daily
- **Cost:** ₹150-250 per liter

### 15. **Garlic Bulb Extract** (వెల్లుల్లి సారం)

- **Cost:** Fresh garlic ₹100-200/kg

## 🥛 FERMENTED ORGANIC INPUTS (Immunity & Nutrition)

### 28. **Panchagavya** (పంచగవ్య)

- **Ingredients:** Cow dung 5kg, cow urine 3L, milk 2L, curd 2L, ghee 1kg, jaggery 3kg, banana 12 nos, tender coconut water 3L
- **Preparation:** Mix all, ferment 15-21 days with daily stirring
- **Application:** 3% foliar spray (30ml/L) every 15 days; 6% soil drench
- **Target:** Plant growth promoter, immunity booster, pest deterrent
- **Mode:** Beneficial microbes, plant hormones, nutrients
- **Shelf Life:** 6 months after fermentation
- **Cost:** ₹400-600 for 20L preparation

### 29. **Jeevamrutham** (జీవామృతం)

- **Ingredients:** Cow dung 10kg, cow urine 10L, jaggery 2kg, pulse flour 2kg, live soil 1 handful, water 200L
- **Preparation:** Mix all, ferment under shade 7 days
- **Application:** Soil drench 200L/acre; 3% foliar spray
- **Target:** Soil microbial boost, plant vigor, disease suppression
- **Mode:** Effective microorganisms proliferation
- **Shelf Life:** Use within 7-10 days
- **Cost:** ₹100-200 per 200L preparation

### 30. **Beejamrutham** (బీజామృతం)

- **Shelf Life:** Use same day
- **Cost:** ₹20-40 per 20L

## ⚗️ TRADITIONAL & MINERAL-BASED REMEDIES

### 34. **Bordeaux Mixture** (బోర్డో మిశ్రమం)

- **Formula:**
    - 1% solution: 1kg CuSO₄ + 1kg quicklime in 100L water
    - 0.5% solution: 500g each in 100L (for tender plants)
- **Preparation:** Dissolve CuSO₄ in 50L water separately, slake lime in 50L water separately, pour CuSO₄ into lime slowly with stirring
- **Application:** Preventive spray before rains; repeat every 10-15 days
- **Target:** Blight, downy mildew, leaf spots, canker, fruit rots
- **Mode:** Copper ions disrupt fungal enzyme systems
- **Shelf Life:** Use same day (cannot store)
- **Caution:** Phytotoxic if> 1% or on tender growth
- **Cost:** ₹150-200 for 100L preparation

### 35. **Burgundy Mixture** (బర్గండీ మిశ్రమం)

- **Formula:** CuSO₄ 1kg + Na₂CO₃ (washing soda) 1.25kg in 100L water
- **Preparation:** Similar to Bordeaux but uses washing soda instead of lime
- **Application:** Same as Bordeaux, safer for dormant season
- **Target:** Same as Bordeaux
- **Advantage:** Less leaf burn than Bordeaux
- **Shelf Life:** Use fresh
- **Cost:** ₹120-180 per 100L

### 36. **Copper Oxychloride** (కాపర్ ఆక్సీక్లోరైడ్)

- **Application:** 3g/L spray

- **Shelf Life:** Spray solution use same day
- **Cost:** ₹80-120 per 100g

## 🌾 PADDY / RICE (వరి) - ALL DISEASES

### FUNGAL DISEASES

#### 1. **Blast** (బ్లాస్ట్)

- **Pathogen:** _Pyricularia oryzae_ (syn. _Magnaporthe oryzae_)
- **Types:** Leaf blast, neck blast, node blast, collar blast
- **Symptoms:**
    - Leaf: Spindle-shaped lesions, gray center, brown margins
    - Neck: Blackening at panicle base, panicle collapse
    - Node: Black lesions at nodes, stem breakage
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity (>90%), cool nights (20-25°C), excess nitrogen, dense planting
- **Land Types:** All (most severe on Alluvial Deltaic)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment (High effectiveness)
    2. Pseudomonas foliar spray (Medium-High)
    3. Bacillus subtilis spray (Medium)
    4. Bordeaux mixture 0.6% (Medium)
    5. Reduce nitrogen fertilization
    6. Maintain proper spacing

#### 2. **Sheath Blight** (ఆవరణ కాలుడు)

- **Pathogen:** _Rhizoctonia solani_
- **Symptoms:** Oval greenish-gray lesions on leaf sheath near waterline, lesions coalesce forming ladder pattern, sclerotia formation
- **Favorable Conditions:** Dense planting, high humidity, stagnant water, high nitrogen
- **Land Types:** Alluvial Deltaic, Black Cotton (waterlogged areas)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bacillus subtilis foliar spray (High)
    2. Pseudomonas spray (Medium)
    3. Jeevamrutham soil application (Medium - improves plant immunity)
    4. Proper drainage management
    5. Reduce plant density
    6. Balanced fertilization

#### 3. **Brown Spot** (గోధుమ మచ్చలు)

- **Pathogen:** _Bipolaris oryzae_ (syn. _Helminthosporium oryzae_)
- **Symptoms:** Round to oval brown spots on leaves and glumes, spots have yellow halo, poor grain filling
- **Favorable Conditions:** Nutrient (especially K) deficiency, drought stress, poor soil
- **Land Types:** Red Sandy Loam (most common due to low fertility)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment (High)
    2. Balanced NPK fertilization (Essential)
    3. Panchagavya spray for nutrition (Medium-High)
    4. Bordeaux mixture (Medium)
    5. Proper water management

#### 4. **Sheath Rot** (ఆవరణ కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Sarocladium oryzae_ (syn. _Acrocylindrium oryzae_)
- **Symptoms:** Sheath rot at boot leaf stage, panicles partially emerge or don't emerge, chaffy grains
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity during booting stage, cloudy weather
- **Land Types:** All
- **Remedies:**
    1. Pseudomonas spray at boot leaf stage (High)
    2. Trichoderma spray (Medium)
    3. Proper drainage
    4. Avoid excess nitrogen during reproductive stage

#### 5. **False Smut** (తప్పుడు పొగమంచు)

- **Pathogen:** _Ustilaginoidea virens_
- **Symptoms:** Individual grains transformed into velvety green to greenish-black spore balls (smut balls)
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity during flowering, rain at flowering
- **Land Types:** Alluvial Deltaic (high humidity areas)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture at boot leaf stage (High)
    2. Pseudomonas spray at panicle initiation (Medium-High)
    3. Proper spacing for air circulation
    4. Avoid excess nitrogen

#### 6. **Stem Rot** (కాండ కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Sclerotium oryzae_
- **Symptoms:** Black lesions on outer leaf sheath near waterline, rotting of stem, lodging, white mycelium inside stem
- **Favorable Conditions:** Deep water, heavy rainfall
- **Land Types:** Alluvial Deltaic (deep water areas)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma soil application (Medium-High)
    2. Proper water management (avoid deep flooding)
    3. Resistant varieties

#### 7. **Bakanae Disease** (బకానే వ్యాధి)

- **Pathogen:** _Fusarium moniliforme_
- **Symptoms:** Abnormally tall, slender pale green plants ("foolish seedling"), sterile or partially filled grains
- **Favorable Conditions:** Infected seeds, alkaline soil
- **Land Types:** Black Cotton Soil (alkaline pH)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment (High)
    2. Pseudomonas seed treatment (High)
    3. Hot water seed treatment (52-54°C for 10 min)
    4. Remove infected plants

#### 8. **Kernel Smut** (గింజ తప్పుడు పొగమంచు)

- **Pathogen:** _Tilletia barclayana_
- **Symptoms:** Infected grains filled with black powdery spores with fishy odor
- **Favorable Conditions:** Cool weather during grain formation
- **Land Types:** All
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed treatment (Medium)
    2. Use certified seeds
    3. Proper field sanitation

#### 9. **Downy Mildew** (కందుపూత)

    1. Trichoderma treatment (High)

## 🍆 BRINJAL / EGGPLANT (వంకాయ) - ALL DISEASES

### FUNGAL DISEASES

#### 93. **Damping Off** (మొలక కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogens:** _Pythium_, _Rhizoctonia_, _Fusarium_
- **Symptoms:** Seedling collapse
- **Favorable Conditions:** Excess moisture
- **Land Types:** All (nursery)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed + soil treatment (High)
    2. Pseudomonas treatment (High)
    3. Raised beds, good drainage

#### 94. **Phomopsis Blight / Fruit Rot** (ఫోమోప్సిస్ కాలుడు / పండు కుళ్ళు)

- **Pathogen:** _Phomopsis vexans_
- **Symptoms:**
    - Seedling: Circular spots on cotyledons, damping off
    - Leaf: Circular to irregular gray spots with concentric rings
    - Stem/petiole: Elongated lesions
    - Fruit: Circular sunken lesions with concentric rings, fruit rot
- **Favorable Conditions:** High humidity, 25-30°C, rain splash
- **Land Types:** All
- **Remedies:**
    1. Bordeaux mixture spray (High)
    2. Trichoderma seed + foliar spray (Medium-High)
    3. Pseudomonas spray (Medium)
    4. Hot water seed treatment (52°C, 30 min)
    5. Remove infected fruits and debris
    6. Proper spacing

#### 95. **Little Leaf / Phyllody** (చిన్న ఆకు / ఫైలోడీ)

- **Pathogen:** Phytoplasma
- **Vector:** Leafhopper (_Hishimonus phycitis_)
- **Symptoms:**
    - Shortening of internodes, bunchy appearance
    - Small, narrow, pale green leaves
    - Flowers turn into leafy structures (phyllody)
    - No fruit formation
- **Favorable Conditions:** High leafhopper population
- **Land Types:** All
- **Remedies:**
    1. Neem oil spray for vector control (Medium)
    2. Remove infected plants immediately
    3. Control leafhopper with neem products
    4. Avoid continuous cropping
    5. Border crops

#### 96. **Fusarium Wilt** (ఫ్యూసేరియమ్ వాడిపోవుట)

- **Pathogen:** _Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. melongenae_
- **Symptoms:** Yellowing, wilting, vascular browning, plant death
- **Favorable Conditions:** High soil temperature, acidic soil
- **Land Types:** Red Sandy Loam (acidic)
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma seed + transplant dip + soil application (High)
    2. Pseudomonas treatment (High)
    3. Crop rotation (3-4 years with non-solanaceous)
    4. Soil solarization
    5. Resistant varieties
    6. Grafting on wild eggplant rootstock

#### 97. **Verticillium Wilt** (వర్టిసిలియమ్ వాడిపోవుట)

- **Pathogen:** _Verticillium dahliae_
- **Symptoms:** Yellowing, wilting, vascular browning
- **Favorable Conditions:** Cool soil temperature
- **Land Types:** All
- **Remedies:**
    1. Trichoderma application (Medium-High)
    2. Crop rotation
    3. Resistant varieties

### BACTERIAL DISEASES

#### 98. **Bacterial Wilt** (బాక్టీరియల్ వాడిపోవుట)

- **Pathogen:** _Ralstonia solanacearum_
- **Symptoms:** Sudden wilting, bacterial ooze from cut stem
- **Favorable Conditions:** High temperature, high moisture
- **Land Types:** Black Cotton Soil, Alluvial
- **Remedies:**
    1. Pseudomonas soil drench + transplant dip (Medium-High)
    2. Bacillus subtilis (Medium)
    3. Remove infected plants
    4. Crop rotation (3-4 years)
    5. Soil solarization

### VIRAL DISEASES

#### 99. **Brinjal Mosaic** (వంకాయ మొజాయిక్)

- **Pathogen:** Multiple viruses (CMV, ToMV, etc.)
- **Vector:** Aphids (CMV), mechanical (ToMV)
- **Symptoms:** Mosaic, mottling, leaf distortion, stunting
- **Land Types:** All
- **Remedies:**
    1. Aphid control with neem oil (Medium)
    2. Remove infected plants
    3. Sanitize tools
    4. Use virus-free seedlings

## 📊 SUMMARY TABLE: DISEASES BY LAND TYPE VULNERABILITY

### Alluvial Deltaic Soils (నదీక్షేత్ర మట్టిలు)

**High Risk:** Blast (Paddy), Sheath blight (Paddy), Brown plant hopper vectors (Paddy viruses), Phytophthora blight (multiple crops)
**Reason:** High moisture, water-logging, high humidity

### Black Cotton Soils (నల్ల మట్టిలు)

**High Risk:** Fusarium wilt (Cotton), Bacterial wilt (Solanaceous crops), Stem rot (_Sclerotium_), Root rots
**Reason:** Alkaline pH, water retention, cracking causing root damage

### Red Sandy Loam (ఎర్ర ఇసుక మట్టిలు)

**High Risk:** Dry root rot (_Macrophomina_), Tikka disease (Groundnut), Collar rot, moisture stress-related diseases
**Reason:** Low water holding capacity, moisture stress, acidic pH

### Mixed Red & Black Soils (మిశ్రమ ఎర్ర & నల్ల మట్టిలు)

**Moderate Risk:** Variable depending on micro-climate and management
**Reason:** Intermediate characteristics

### Hill Plateau Soils (కొండ పీఠభూమి మట్టిలు)

**High Risk:** Blast (paddy in valleys), Leaf blights, Rusts (cooler temperatures favor)
**Reason:** High humidity in valleys, cool temperatures, undulating terrain with moisture pockets
